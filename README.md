# LLMs – the Good, the Bad or the Indispensable?: A Use Case on Legal Statute Prediction and Legal Judgment Prediction on Indian Court Cases
This repository contains the relevant data and codes for the paper 'LLMs – the Good, the Bad or the Indispensable?: A Use Case on Legal Statute Prediction and Legal Judgment Prediction on Indian Court Cases' accepted to the Findings of EMNLP 2023 conference.
